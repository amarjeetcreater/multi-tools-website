Color Theory
  • Monochromatic: Same hue, different shades
  • Complementary: Opposite colors on wheel
  • Analogous: Adjacent colors on wheel
  • Triadic: Three colors 120° apart
  • Tetradic: Two pairs of complementary colors
  • Split Complementary: Base + two adjacent to complement
Design Tips
  • • Use 60-30-10 rule for color distribution
  • • Consider color psychology
  • • Test accessibility (WCAG guidelines)
  • • Use color for hierarchy
  • • Consider cultural associations
  • • Test in different lighting
