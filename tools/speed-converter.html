Speed Information

Common Speed References:
  • Walking: 5 km/h (3 mph)
  • Running: 12 km/h (7.5 mph)
  • City Driving: 50 km/h (31 mph)
  • Highway Speed: 120 km/h (75 mph)
  • Commercial Aircraft: 900 km/h (560 mph)
  • Speed of Sound: 1,235 km/h (767 mph)
Speed Units Explained:
  • km/h: Kilometers per hour - metric speed unit
  • mph: Miles per hour - imperial speed unit
  • m/s: Meters per second - SI speed unit
  • knots: Nautical miles per hour - maritime/aviation
  • Mach: Speed relative to speed of sound
  • Speed of Light: 299,792,458 m/s
