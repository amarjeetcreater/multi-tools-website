Data Storage Information

Common Storage References:
  • Text File: 1-10 KB
  • Photo: 1-10 MB
  • MP3 Song: 3-5 MB
  • HD Video: 100-500 MB
  • DVD Movie: 4-8 GB
  • Blu-ray Movie: 25-50 GB
  • Hard Drive: 1-20 TB
  • Data Center: 1-100 PB
Storage Units Explained:
  • Decimal Units: Based on powers of 10 (KB, MB, GB)
  • Binary Units: Based on powers of 2 (KiB, MiB, GiB)
  • Bytes: 8 bits = 1 byte
  • Kilobytes: 1,000 bytes (decimal) or 1,024 bytes (binary)
  • Megabytes: 1,000 KB (decimal) or 1,024 KB (binary)
  • Gigabytes: 1,000 MB (decimal) or 1,024 MB (binary)
  • Bits: Smallest unit of digital data
  • Nibble: 4 bits = 0.5 bytes

Conversion Types

Decimal (SI) Units:
  • 1 KB = 1,000 bytes
  • 1 MB = 1,000 KB = 1,000,000 bytes
  • 1 GB = 1,000 MB = 1,000,000,000 bytes
  • 1 TB = 1,000 GB = 1,000,000,000,000 bytes
Binary (IEC) Units:
  • 1 KiB = 1,024 bytes
  • 1 MiB = 1,024 KiB = 1,048,576 bytes
  • 1 GiB = 1,024 MiB = 1,073,741,824 bytes
  • 1 TiB = 1,024 GiB = 1,099,511,627,776 bytes
