How to Download Twitter Videos
  1. Find the tweet containing the video you want to download
  2. Click the share button and copy the tweet URL
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above
  4. Click "Get Video" to load the video
  5. Select your preferred quality
  6. Click "Download Video" to save it to your device
