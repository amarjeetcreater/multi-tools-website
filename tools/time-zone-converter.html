Time Zone Information

Major Time Zones
  • UTC - Coordinated Universal Time (World standard)
  • EST/EDT - Eastern Time (US East Coast)
  • PST/PDT - Pacific Time (US West Coast)
  • GMT/BST - London Time
  • JST - Japan Standard Time
Daylight Saving Time
  • Spring Forward: Clocks move ahead 1 hour
  • Fall Back: Clocks move back 1 hour
  • Not all countries observe DST
  • Dates vary by country
  • Affects time zone conversions
