Length Measurement Systems

Metric System
  • Millimeter (mm) - 1/1000 of a meter
  • Centimeter (cm) - 1/100 of a meter
  • Meter (m) - Base unit of length
  • Kilometer (km) - 1000 meters
  • Used worldwide, except in USA
Imperial System
  • Inch (in) - 1/12 of a foot
  • Foot (ft) - 12 inches
  • Yard (yd) - 3 feet
  • Mile (mi) - 5280 feet
  • Primarily used in USA
