Weight Measurement Systems

Metric System
  • Milligram (mg) - 1/1000 of a gram
  • Gram (g) - Base unit of weight
  • Kilogram (kg) - 1000 grams
  • Metric Ton (t) - 1000 kilograms
  • Used worldwide, except in USA
Imperial System
  • Grain (gr) - Smallest unit
  • Ounce (oz) - 437.5 grains
  • Pound (lb) - 16 ounces
  • Stone (st) - 14 pounds
  • Primarily used in USA and UK

Interesting Weight Facts

Precision Measurements
  • Diamonds measured in carats
  • Gold measured in troy ounces
  • Medications in milligrams
  • Precious metals in grams
  • Large cargo in metric tons
Conversion Tips
  • 1 kg ≈ 2.2 pounds
  • 1 pound ≈ 0.45 kg
  • 1 ounce ≈ 28.35 grams
  • 1 gram ≈ 0.035 ounces
  • 1 stone = 14 pounds
