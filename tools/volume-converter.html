Volume Information

Common Volume References:
  • Water Bottle: 500 mL (0.5 L)
  • Milk Jug: 1 gallon (3.785 L)
  • Gas Tank: 15 gallons (56.8 L)
  • Swimming Pool: 20,000 gallons (75,700 L)
  • Olympic Pool: 660,000 gallons (2.5 million L)
  • Teaspoon: 5 mL
  • Tablespoon: 15 mL
  • Cup: 240 mL
Volume Units Explained:
  • Liters: Metric volume unit (1 L = 1000 mL)
  • US Gallons: US customary unit (1 gal = 3.785 L)
  • Imperial Gallons: UK unit (1 imp gal = 4.546 L)
  • Cubic Meters: SI volume unit (1 m³ = 1000 L)
  • Cubic Feet: Imperial volume unit (1 ft³ = 28.317 L)
  • Pints: 1 US pint = 473 mL, 1 Imperial pint = 568 mL
  • Cups: 1 US cup = 240 mL
  • Fluid Ounces: 1 US fl oz = 29.574 mL
