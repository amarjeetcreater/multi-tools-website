About Authority Scores

Domain Authority (DA) and Page Authority (PA) are SEO metrics that predict how likely a website or page is to rank in search engines.

  • 0-30: New or low-authority sites
  • 31-50: Established sites
  • 51-70: High-authority sites
  • 71-100: Very high-authority sites
