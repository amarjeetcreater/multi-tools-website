Unit Explanations

The SI unit of area. One square meter is the area of a square with sides of 1 meter.

Common unit in the US. One square foot is the area of a square with sides of 1 foot.

Traditional unit for land area. One acre equals 43,560 square feet or about 4,047 square meters.

Area Tips

  • Use square meters for scientific calculations
  • Square feet common in real estate
  • Acres used for agricultural land
  • Hectares common in Europe
