How to Download Facebook Videos
  1. Find the Facebook video you want to download
  2. Click the share button and copy the video URL
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above
  4. Click "Get Video" to load the video
  5. Select your preferred quality (HD or SD)
  6. Click "Download Video" to save it to your device
Supported Video Types
  • Public Facebook videos
  • Facebook Watch videos
  • Facebook page videos
  • Facebook live videos (after they end)
