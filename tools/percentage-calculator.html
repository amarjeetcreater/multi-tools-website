Percentage Calculator

Calculate percentages, find percentages of numbers, and perform percentage increase/decrease calculations.

Percentage Tips
  • Basic Formula: (Part ÷ Whole) × 100
  • Increase: ((New - Original) ÷ Original) × 100
  • Decrease: ((Original - New) ÷ Original) × 100
  • Percentage Of: (Percentage ÷ 100) × Base Number
